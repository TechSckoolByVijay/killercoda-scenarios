## 💡 Daily Practice Tips

**Level Up Your Navigation:**
- **Use `ls -lah`** as your default directory view
- **Practice Tab completion** to speed up your workflow
- **Combine pwd + ls + cd** for confident navigation
- **Try different path types** to become flexible

**Real-World Scenarios:**
- **Finding log files:** `cd /var/log && ls -lth` (newest logs first)
- **Project exploration:** `cd ~/projects && find . -name "*.js"`
- **Quick workspace setup:** `cd ~ && ls -la` (see your environment)
